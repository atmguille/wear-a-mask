Welcome to WearAMask!

This simple website has been made with the purpose of highlighting the importance of using a mask. Through executing the simulation with different combinations of variables, the user should realize that populations where mask percentage is higher take more epochs to get fully infected than others. Also, social distance (board size) is another key factor.

The population is randomly distributed and moves randomly. The probability of infection, which is applied when two people are closer than the minimum safe distance, can be found below in the image. For more details, visit the GitHub project

Enjoy... and #WearAMask
